Kitchen Cleaning

  • Take out garbage and recycling

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Bathroom Cleaning
  • Wash and sanitize the toilet, shower, tub and sink
  • Dust all accessible surfaces
  • Wipe down all mirrors and glass fixtures

Carpet Cleaning

  • Meeting with clients to discuss their cleaning needs.
  • Assessing the state of the floors and carpets that need to be cleaned.
  • Applying stain and pre-cleaning treatments to the carpets and floors.
  • Carefully removing furniture to access all areas that need to be cleaned.

Sofa Cleaning

  • Vacuum the sofa to remove any loose dirt or debris .
  • Clean the wood or metal areas of the sofa using a cleaning solution and a clean cloth .
  • Spot clean any stains or spills using a cleaning solution and a clean cloth .
  • Deep clean the sofa using a steam cleaner or hot water extraction method
