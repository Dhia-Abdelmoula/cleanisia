About

Cleanisia is a professional house cleaning service that is dedicated to providing high-quality cleaning services to homeowners. Our team of experienced cleaners is committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed our clients’ expectations. We use environmentally friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets. At Cleanisia, we believe that a clean home is a happy home, and we are passionate about helping our clients maintain a clean and healthy living environment.

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Dust all accessible surfaces
  • Empty sink and load up dishwasher with dirty dishes
  • Wipe down exterior of stove, oven and fridge
  • Clean all floor surfaces
  • Take out garbage and recycling

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Bathroom Cleaning
  • Wash and sanitize the toilet, shower, tub and sink
  • Dust all accessible surfaces
  • Wipe down all mirrors and glass fixtures
  • Clean all floor surfaces

Carpet Cleaning

  • Meeting with clients to discuss their cleaning needs.
  • Assessing the state of the floors and carpets that need to be cleaned.
  • Applying stain and pre-cleaning treatments to the carpets and floors.
  • Carefully removing furniture to access all areas that need to be cleaned.

Sofa Cleaning

  • Vacuum the sofa to remove any loose dirt or debris .
  • Clean the wood or metal areas of the sofa using a cleaning solution and a clean cloth .
  • Spot clean any stains or spills using a cleaning solution and a clean cloth .
  • Deep clean the sofa using a steam cleaner or hot water extraction method

Floor Cleaning

  • Dust all accessible surfaces
  • Empty sink and load up dishwasher with dirty dishes
  • Wipe down exterior of stove, oven and fridge
  • Clean all floor surfaces
  • Take out garbage and recycling

Window Cleaning

  • Wipe the window dry using a clean, rubber-bladed squeegee.
